# WellTegra & WellArk User Manual

Welcome to the **WellTegra & WellArk** ecosystem—a forensic, physics-anchored audit platform for oil & gas assets. This manual provides a detailed overview of all features and how to use them to maintain forensic authority over your well data.

---

## 1. Executive Dashboard
The Dashboard provides an immediate "Ground Truth" overview of the active well program.
- **Key Hazards**: Displays critical risks identified in the program and forensic audits.
- **Financials**: Real-time tracking of AFE (Authorized for Expenditure) vs. Accrued Costs.
- **Personnel**: Quick view of assigned roles and responsible parties.

## 2. Well Identification & Data
This section is organized into sub-tabs for forensic precision:
- **Well Header**: Manage basic identification (UWI, Rig, Program Title).
- **Well Trajectory**: Surface coordinates and water depth data.
- **Casing Schematics**: Define casing strings (Size, Weight, Grade, Shoe Depths).
- **Tubing Schematics**: Specify production tubing details and setting depths.
- **Reservoir Data**: Forensic tracking of pressure, temperature, and fluid contacts (OOWC).
- **Well Status**: Monitor SIHWP, suspension details, and real-time tree valve positions (Open/Closed).

## 3. Operational Workflow
- **Operational Phases**: A step-by-step breakdown of the intervention program.
- **Status Indicators**: Visual cues for 'Complete', 'Active', and 'Next' phases.
- **Procedures**: Detailed forensic instructions for each phase of the operation.

## 4. Analogue Intelligence & Lessons Learned
- **Historical Comparison**: Review insights from the 6 analogue wells provided in the site study.
- **Lesson Injection**: Directly copy lessons learned from historical analogues into your current program to avoid repeating past failures.

## 5. Wellbore Schematic & Engineering Calculators
- **2D Schematic**: Interactive visualization of the wellbore. Hover over items to see forensic details.
- **Engineering Calculators**: Integrated tools for calculating hydrostatic pressure, kill mud weights, and volume displacements based on current well data.

## 6. 3D Wellbore Visualization
- **Deviation Survey**: Input MD, Inclination, and Azimuth data manually or via CSV upload.
- **Path Rendering**: Interactive 3D model of the well path to visualize doglegs and potential mechanical risks.

## 7. Job Execution Analysis
- **Log Analysis**: Upload high-frequency job logs (CSV) to visualize trip speeds and mechanical performance.
- **Forensic Delta**: Identify discrepancies between planned vs. actual operational speeds.

## 8. Live Operations & AI Anomaly Detection
- **Real-Time Monitoring**: Review daily reports and event logs as they happen.
- **AI Anomaly Detector**: Leverages the **WellTegra Harvester** (Gemini AI) to identify forensic inconsistencies or physics-defying sensor data in real-time.

## 9. Document & Data Management
- **Forensic Repository**: Upload PDFs, LAS files, and reports.
- **AI Context**: Uploaded documents are automatically indexed by the AI to provide context-aware answers to forensic queries.

## 10. Risk & Compliance
- **Hazards & Mitigations**: Detailed tracking of operational risks.
- **Management of Change (MOC)**: Formal workflow for documenting and approving deviations from the original plan.
- **Permits**: Status tracking for all required regulatory permissions.

## 11. Resources & Logistics
- **Fluids & Materials**: Manage volumes, purposes, and permit status for all wellbore fluids.
- **Equipment**: Inventory tracking for critical operational hardware.

## 12. Document Approvals
- **Sign-off Workflow**: Formal authorization log including roles, names, and digital signatures to ensure accountability.

---

## Forensic Authority Rules
1. **The Immutable Source Rule**: Every piece of data has a "Provenance Tooltip" showing its origin (e.g., NSTA API vs. WellTegra Forensic Engine).
2. **Physics Anchoring**: All calculations are validated against mass-energy balance rules. If data defies physics, the **WellArk** map will glow red.
